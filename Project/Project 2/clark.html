Welome to Clark Athletic Center

Clark Athletic Center host all the games on campus. Here, you can find the ice rink, baseball field, and the basketball court. There is also a varsity only gym as well. Every week there are open gyms and rink if students would like to participate. It is a fun place to be to watch UMass games and to support the teams.
