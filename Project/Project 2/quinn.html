Welcome to Quinn Administration Building

Quinn Administration Building is a resources place to go. Here, you have access to Transportation Services, University Health Services, and College of Nursing. This is a place where you go to handle things such as parking passes, health records, checkups, etc. Right next to the main building is the UMass Police Office.
