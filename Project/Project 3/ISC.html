Welcome to the Integrated Science Complex

The Integrated Science Complex is probably the best looking building on campus. Here, people conduct research and labs. If you're a student who takes classes such as Biology or Chemistry, you can expect your labs to take place here. There's also a Starbucks here, so if you feel hungry, thirsty, or both, you have easy access here. This building is often quiet and has nice places to sit and do work.
