Welcome to University Hall

Univeristy Hall is another one of the newer buildings. This is one of the more popular buildings to be in. Due to its nice design and classes, many students find it enjoyable to have classes here. Here in University Hall otherwise known as "U-Hall," there is an open area to study, and also a food court. Located on the second floor, the food court is a great place to eat at when you are hungry.
