Welcome! This self-navigating website allows users, whom we intend to be Umass Boston students/faculty, to gain an insight as to what the schools offers and everything around the beautiful city, of Boston, that surrounds it. This website has four main pages in which helpful buildings, fun attractions, and contacts of us three are provided. Despite the website's straightforward manuever, certain pages have unique features such as the slideshow and flip-card features! When on the "Places Near Me" page, this will provide a myriad of attractions, from fun places around Boston to delicious food attractions to try when on a study break! Lastly, the contact page will allow any user to have the ability to contact us three if ever wished or needed and a picture of each of us too; it is always nice to have a face to a name! We want our website to be portrayed as a personal and welcoming platform for all fellow beacons and hope to encourage an even tighter-knit community!
