Hover over any of the below attractions and get an insight into the fun adventures you can explore right near campus!

Musuem of Science

This exhibit has many interactive aspects. One that is very famous is the simulation exhibit in which participants can sit in a virtual setting and be taken soaring across the world to different tourist attractions with 4D effects.

Harbor Walk

Right by campus you can walk alongside a sidewalk with the beautiful view of the ocean! It is a great way to get in some exercise in a place right near campus in between classes!

Anh Hong

Anh Hong is a Viet-Food-Based restaurant. It is a cute place to try different vietnamese food/dishes! Here is a link to the restaurnt's website: Anh Hong website

Umass Shuttles

Shuttle #1 = Campus Center, JKF/Umass Train Station

Shuttle #2 = Campus Center, Umass Dorms, Bayside Parking, ISC

Some apps that students use in order to get exact/current times of when shuttles run include: Rider or even their Google/Apple Maps app.

JFK Musuem

The JFK Musuem is a famous attraction known to honor our 35th President: John F. Kennedy. Inside, there are artifacts, pictures, and signed objects where tourists can enjoy. This is a great place to take history classes--in fact, many Umass classes make a trip to this library during their class times

Musuem of Fine Arts

This is primarily an arts museum in which art, such as paintings, are displayed. Many of the paintings are famous artistis from all different countries around the world. Many foreign art pieces are visable here.

Boston Children's Musuem

This is a children's museum in Boston dedicated to expanding education for children. They have activties having to deal with all subjects; one good example is an exhibit they have which showcases Newton's Laws in Physics which many college students still expand their learning on.
