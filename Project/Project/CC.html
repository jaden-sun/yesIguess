Welcome to Campus Center

Campus Center is one of the newer buildings of UMass Boston. This building offers many services that students can use. Students have access to the Food Court whenever they feel the need to eat food. Also, there is Campus Store if students want to purchase UMass Boston Wear, snacks, rentals, supplies, electronics, and more. Many will find this store to be useful. Campus Center contains many things that would help a student. Places such as the Bursar's Office, the One-Stop, or even the game room for entertainment. You'll find that many events are held here in this building. Most events would usually be held in the ballrooms in the upper levels. After a long day of classes or other things you might do, shuttle buses are available in front of the building that run until 1:00 AM.
